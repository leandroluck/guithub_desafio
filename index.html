Get to konw me

My name is Flavia Luck and I am a professional Graphic Designer with more than 10 years of experience. I have worked for Latin American companies, and as I am currently living in China, also in the local design field.

I have studied Visual Communication Design and have specialized in Design Theory and Criticism at Buenos Aires University (UBA), Argentina. I have recently got my master's degrees at the China Academy of Art (CAA) under the tutoring of designer Bi (毕学锋）and I am currently at the Shanghai University- Academy of Arts in the Design Science Ph.D. program under the tutoring of artist Bo (金奖皮 ).

I specialize in illustration and contemporary character design with Kawaii aesthetic. I also work for smaller-scale promotional media, printed books, magazines, and merchants. I also collab in some projects with DITART©️, an Argentinean industrial designer entrepreneur with pretty interesting ideas and a 3D printing machine. From the academic side, I teach art and design history for Chinese spoken students and Chinese language for Spanish spoken students.

Due to the experiened mentioned before, I am currently working as a Chinese Language Teacher (begginer and advanced levels) at the Confucius Institute - La Plata National University, Buenos Aires, Argentina.
